The Impigrity Catalog

I have personally repented and ranted a lot on doing some repetitive manual work. With all frustrations, I use to spend some time on figuring out some ways to prevent doing repetitive manual work. So that I could avoid inefficient and non productive approaches. The catalog of Impigrity which I recently started is a place where I publish my ways of getting things done with Impigrity.
